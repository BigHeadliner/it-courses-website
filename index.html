About the course

If you are looking for a creative modern profession then this course is for you. We will teach you how create websites and mobile applications, work with video and animation, draw illustrations. You will learn how to work in 3D: create characters, game locations, interiors and exteriors. We will help you master all the necessary tools from scratch.

How education goes

Comfortable classes

We have many comfortable classrooms, conference rooms and laboratories. You will have simple workplace with a personal computer.

Modern equipment

Our partners: Cisco, Microsoft, Avtodesk, world leaders in the field of IT. You will have everything necessary hardware and software.

Work with teachers

Our teachers are practicing designers. At every lesson you are together you will analyze topics, complete tasks, analyze real cases and projects.

Lectures, webinars and workshops

We often invite well-known experts from the industry that become useful knowledge and secrets of their work.

Work with real projects

You will have the opportunity to understand and work on real projects for more stage of learning.

Making a portfolio

During the training period, you will collect your own portfolio of completed assignments, course and diploma projects.
